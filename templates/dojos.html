CoderDojo Silicon Valley is made up of independently run dojos located in Mountain View and Campbell, California.

There are other dojos in the Bay Area located in San Francisco and Dublin, California, in addition to hundreds of CoderDojos around the world!

Each dojo has it's own format and style. Some run one once a month, others more. Find the location and schedule that fits you best, or attend them all.

At CoderDojo Silicon Valley, there's always something interesting taking place and many ways to Get Involved.

We welcome new coders at any time, and we hope to see you soon at a dojo near you!

Can anyone start a CoderDojo?

Absolutely! Become a Champion and change the world! Read more about starting your own dojo.

If you really want to give it a go, don’t do it alone. Ask us, and we’ll be happy to tell you all about our local start up process and specific lessons learned. Contact Us, if you have any questions.
