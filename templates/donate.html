Donate

Tax-deductible donations can be made to CoderDojo Silicon Valley (CDSV) through our fiscal sponsor.

How to donate

By check

Make check payable to: 

Silicon Valley Education Foundation

In the memo line of your check, be sure to include the following: 

FBO/CoderDojo Silicon Valley

Mail checks to:

Marcy Delgado
PO BOX 20927
San Jose CA 95160

Online*

Use the SVEF online donation form. Select this option:

I prefer to donate a different amount

In the Comments/Cause section of the form, be sure to include the following: 

FBO/CoderDojo Silicon Valley

If your employer matches contributions, enter their name in the section on the form: 

Employer's Name

90% of all donations go directly to CDSV programs. SVEF retains 10% as compensation for donation processing and tax reporting services.

*If you donate online, an additional 5% is applied towards PayPal/credit card fees.
